Work bio

Ulf is a master student of Digital Media Engineering at the Technical University of Denmark, currently working on his master thesis at the Weizmann Institute of Science in Israel, with the Uri Alon Lab, on using Archetypal Analysis to understand how smartphone usage patterns link to basic psychological profiles. In the past he has worked part time as Data Scientist at Trustpilot developing fraud detection systems, as an animation artist with Sterlitech and as a teaching assistant at DTU in the courses Physics 1 and Computational Tools for Big Data. His academic background is within the field of Physics and Nanotechnology, where he has co-authored work on membrane module optimization. Interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, fraud detection, data visualization, open data, Computational Social Science, Graph Theory, and algorithms.
