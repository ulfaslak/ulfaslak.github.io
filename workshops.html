Workshop quote configurator

I run workshops and courses for companies on various applied topics. My rate depends on many factors. This configurator gives you a cost estimate and breakdown. The quote is accurate but non-binding, and the final price is determined by the actual scope.

The base price includes up to 12 people per cohort. Above that, the quote adds a small per-person amount for facilitation load, exercise support, and follow-up questions. 12 people
A cohort is one run-through of the workshop. If you have 36 participants, one cohort means one large workshop; two cohorts means I run the workshop for 18 participants twice. Each extra cohort adds 50% of the live-workshop price because the material is already prepared, but teaching time increases. 1 group
