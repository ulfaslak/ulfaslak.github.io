I help teams build better statistical, machine-learning, and AI systems, and I teach the parts that make those systems usable in practice.

I offer

  • Consulting: Bayesian marketing mix modeling, generative AI, machine learning, statistical inference, and data visualization. Mainly through PyMC Labs. I work best on difficult, well-defined problems, from production MMMs (e.g. Precis Digital's Alvie, serving 20+ major brands) to one-off applied math/ML work and open-source scientific software.
  • Talks and workshops: I run practical sessions for companies on applied topics. Common ones: context engineering & agent-assisted development for engineering teams, Bayesian marketing mix modeling, generative AI for business aimed at non-technical leadership who want a working mental model, and Python & modern data science for teams transitioning away from MATLAB, SAS, Stata, or Excel.

For teaching requests, the fastest way to start is to sketch a rough scope with the workshop quote builder.
